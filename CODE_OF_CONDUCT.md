# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and collaborative space
for everyone participating in the Auromake community.

This community exists to share knowledge, ask questions, and learn together.

## Expected Behavior

We encourage -
- Respectful and considerate communication
- Constructive feedback and thoughtful discussion
- Patience with differing skill levels and perspectives
- Positive intent and good faith participation

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are not acceptable -
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Hate speech, abusive language, or intimidation
- Spam, self-promotion without context, or disruptive behavior
- Sharing private or sensitive information without consent

## Moderation

Community maintainers reserve the right to -
- Remove or edit content that violates this Code of Conduct
- Temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary

Moderation decisions are made to protect the health and integrity of the community.

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or observe behavior that violates this Code of Conduct,
please report it privately via the official Auromake contact channels.

Thank you for helping keep this community constructive and welcoming.
